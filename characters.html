Characters in the Fall of Icarus

Character Description Image
Icarus The ambitious son of Daedalus who, despite his father's warnings, flew too close to the sun with wings made of feathers and wax.
Daedalus A skilled inventor and father of Icarus, who created the labyrinth for King Minos of Crete and crafted the wings that led to his son's downfall.
King Midas The greedy king granted the golden touch by Dionysus, turning everything he touched into gold, including his own daughter.
Minotaur A monstrous creature, half man and half bull, imprisoned within the labyrinth constructed by Daedalus, serving as a terrifying guardian.
